Aura Points are the currency of Aura Farm. Every time you complete a challenge, you earn points that reflect how actively you're engaging with your campus community. Your total determines your tier, your rank on the leaderboard, and the color of your aura.

## Earning points

Points are awarded automatically when you successfully submit a challenge completion. The number of points you receive depends on the challenge's **points reward** — different challenges are worth different amounts, and harder challenges tend to award more.

There is no other way to earn or lose points. Your total only ever goes up.

## Progression tiers

Your points total determines which aura tier you're in. There are six tiers, each with its own color:

| Tier | Color | Minimum points |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Purple | 0 pts |
| 2 | Blue | 25 pts |
| 3 | Green | 75 pts |
| 4 | Yellow | 150 pts |
| 5 | Orange | 300 pts |
| 6 | Red | 500 pts |

You start at purple and progress upward as you accumulate points. The Aura screen shows your current tier color and your overall rank.

## Progress bar

The home screen displays a progress bar that shows how far you've come toward the next tier threshold. Once you reach a threshold, the bar resets and tracks progress toward the following tier. At the top tier (red, 500 pts), the bar reflects your position within that tier.

<Tip>
  Check the progress bar after each completion to see how close you are to your next tier jump.
</Tip>

## Daily streaks

Alongside your total points, Aura Farm tracks a daily streak — the number of consecutive calendar days on which you've completed at least one challenge.

Streak rules:

- **First completion ever** — your streak starts at 1.
- **Multiple completions in one day** — your streak does not increase beyond 1 for that day. Completing more challenges in a single day builds points, not streaks.
- **Completing on the next calendar day** — your streak increments by 1.
- **Missing a day** — your streak resets to 1 on your next completion.

<Note>
  Streaks are calculated using UTC calendar days. A "day" ends at midnight UTC regardless of your local timezone.
</Note>

Your current streak appears alongside your rank on the leaderboard, so consistent daily activity is rewarded even if your total points are modest.

## Viewing your aura

The **Aura** tab shows your current tier as a colored diamond icon, a label describing your aura color, and your global rank. You can share a screenshot of your aura directly from this screen.
