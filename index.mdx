Aura Farm rewards students for real-world actions like attending events, volunteering, hiking, and exploring campus. Complete location-based challenges, submit photo proof, earn Aura points, and climb the leaderboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create an account with your email address and verify it with a one-time code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse challenges">
    Open the app to see available challenges near you — each with a location, difficulty, and Aura point reward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete a challenge">
    Head to the location, take a photo as proof, add a caption, and submit. Your GPS coordinates are verified automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Aura and climb the ranks">
    Points update instantly. Maintain daily streaks and compete on the global leaderboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
