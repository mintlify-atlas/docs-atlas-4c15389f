Aura Farm is a lighthearted mobile game for students that rewards real-world actions like attending events, volunteering, hiking, and exploring campus. Complete location-based challenges, submit photo proof, earn Aura points, and climb the leaderboard.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create an account with your email address. You'll receive a verification code to confirm your identity before you can start playing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse challenges">
    Open the app to see available challenges near you. Each challenge has a location, difficulty rating, and Aura point reward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete a challenge">
    Head to the challenge location, take a photo as proof, add a caption, and submit. Your GPS coordinates are verified automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Aura and climb the ranks">
    Your Aura points are updated instantly. Maintain daily streaks for bonus momentum and compete with other students on the global leaderboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

- **Location-based verification** — GPS coordinates are checked at submission to confirm you were physically present
- **Aura point tiers** — Progress through five tiers (25 → 75 → 150 → 300 → 500 pts) as you complete more challenges
- **Social feed** — See what other students are completing, like their posts, and get inspired
- **Daily streaks** — Complete at least one challenge per day to keep your streak alive
- **Community moderation** — Flag suspicious completions to help keep the leaderboard fair
